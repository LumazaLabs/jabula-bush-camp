We only sell wood, briquettes, ice and firelighters but there is a general and bottle store only 3km's away.

NO, we are completely off the grid and only make use of solar, gas and borehole water.

We do not, our staff stay off the reserve but cleaning can be arranged at an extra cost. Available every day except for Sundays & Public Holidays. Please not you must inform us at least a day prior to when you want the cleaning done.

We are situated within the Dinokeng Game Reserve which does have the Big 5. The chances of you seeing animals from your tent are good as we have watering holes nearby, however it is just about the luck of the draw.

4x Adults & 2x Children (Under 12YRS)

There are two bedrooms each with en-suite bathrooms as well as a sleeper couch suitable for children under 12YRS.

You pay per tent per night regardless of the number of people.

We do not allow pets as we are situated within the Game Reserve and so is dangerous for them.
